FIP is now a curable disease!

FIP is no longer a terminal diagnosis for cats. It's true! If you have a cat with FIP, please immediately post on the FIP Warrior facebook group, and an admin will be in touch shortly to help you treat your precious floof.

How much does it cost?

Although FIP is now curable, the treatment is not inexpensive. Costs will depend on your cat's weight and other symptoms. Typically, costs vary from around $2,500 to $8,000 for the duration of treatment.

Scientific Basis?

Researchers at the University of California-Davis have spent decades researching FIP. Up until their groundbreaking study that came out in 2019, FIP was largely regarded as a terminal disease in cats, with most vets recommending euthanasia. Since mid-2019, tens-of-thousands of cat owners everywhere have been able to cure their cats of FIP.
